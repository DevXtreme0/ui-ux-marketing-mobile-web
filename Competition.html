If you won,

  • Product - will eligible for get free winning products from the supermarket.

    Final points calculation for to be top three in leaderboard

  • 750 points to 799 points = Entry Level
  • 4,000 points to 7,999 points = Bronze Tier
  • 8,000 points to 29,999 points = Silver Tier
  • 30,000 points to 39,999 points = Gold Tier
  • 40,000 points to 49,999 points = Crystal Tier
  • 50,000 points above = Legendary Tier

    Top three players with higher points in relevant tier points will be awarded product discounts
